What services are included in business compliance solutions?

Business compliance solutions generally include GST registration and return filing, income tax compliance, business registrations, statutory filings, regulatory advisory, and documentation support to help businesses meet legal requirements.

When does a business need GST registration in India?

A business usually needs GST registration when its annual turnover exceeds the prescribed threshold or when it conducts interstate sales, e-commerce transactions, or supplies through online marketplaces.

What are the common compliance requirements for startups in India?

Startups typically require GST registration, PAN and TAN registration, income tax filings, MSME registration, and periodic compliance filings, depending on their business structure and operations.

Why do businesses outsource tax and compliance services?

Many businesses outsource tax and compliance services to ensure accuracy, reduce regulatory risks, stay updated with changing laws, and save time that can be focused on core business activities.

What is the difference between GST filing and income tax filing?

GST filing relates to reporting sales, purchases, and tax liabilities under the Goods and Services Tax system, while income tax filing reports the overall income and tax liability of individuals or businesses for a financial year.

What documents are usually required for business registrations?

Common documents include PAN, Aadhaar, business address proof, bank account details, identity proof of promoters, and relevant business information, depending on the registration type.

What is MSME registration, and why is it beneficial?

MSME (Udyam) registration allows small and medium enterprises to access government benefits such as subsidies, easier loan approvals, tax incentives, and participation in government procurement programs.

What is the Government e-Marketplace (GeM)?

The Government e-Marketplace (GeM) is an online platform where businesses can sell products and services directly to government departments and public sector organisations.

What role do APIs play in business automation?

APIs allow businesses to integrate systems such as GST portals, KYC verification platforms, and financial databases with their internal software to automate compliance and operational processes.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is required to register for GST in India?

Businesses must register for GST if their turnover exceeds the prescribed limit or if they supply goods or services across states, sell online, or fall under mandatory registration categories.

How long does GST registration take after applying?

GST registration generally takes a few working days after submission of the correct documents, depending on verification by the department.

What returns need to be filed under GST?

Most businesses are required to file GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, and annual returns, while the frequency depends on turnover and scheme.

What happens if GST returns are filed late?

Late filing may result in penalties, interest charges, and restrictions on input tax credit until returns are filed.

What documents are needed for GST registration?

Common documents include PAN, Aadhaar, business address proof, bank details, and photographs of promoters or proprietors.

Can a small business operate without GST registration?

If the business turnover is below the threshold and not under mandatory registration, GST may not be required. However, eligibility must be checked carefully.

What is input tax credit, and why is reconciliation important?

Input tax credit allows businesses to claim tax paid on purchases. Reconciliation ensures that the credit matches GST portal records.

Can GST notices be handled professionally?

Yes, GST notices can be replied to with proper documentation and explanation to avoid penalties or further action.

Who needs to file an income tax return in India?

Individuals and businesses earning taxable income during a financial year are required to file an income tax return, even if tax is already deducted.

What documents are required for income tax filing?

What is the due date for filing income tax returns?

For most individuals, the due date is usually 31 July unless extended by the government.

What happens if an income tax return is not filed?

Can income tax returns be revised after filing?

Yes, revised returns can be filed within the allowed time if errors are found.

What is TDS and why is it important?

TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) is tax deducted on payments such as salary, rent, or professional fees, and must be reported correctly.
